<h1 align="center" style="color: #2E86C1;">🗺️ Welcome to the Server: Player Survival Guide</h1>

Welcome! This server runs a custom Fabric modpack that significantly overhauls vanilla survival, combat, and exploration. To help you survive your first few nights and prepare for the endgame, here is everything you need to know about the changes to the world.

---

<h2 style="color: #E67E22;">🌡️ 1. Core Survival Mechanics (The Elements)</h2>

The environment is now just as dangerous as the mobs. You will need to prepare for the elements before venturing out.

* **Temperature System:** The world features dynamic temperature mechanics. You can freeze to death in cold biomes (**Frostiful**) or overheat in deserts and the Nether (**Scorchful**). You must dress appropriately, use campfires to stay warm, or utilize cooling items to survive the heat.
* **Dynamic Seasons:** The world experiences changing seasons (**Serene Seasons**). This will affect crop growth rates, weather patterns, and the ambient temperature, so stockpile food for the winter!
* **Enhanced Healing:** Keep an eye on your food types. You can clearly see how much hunger and saturation a food item will restore before you eat it (**AppleSkin**).

---

<h2 style="color: #C0392B;">⚔️ 2. RPG Systems & Combat</h2>

Forget vanilla sword-spamming; combat and character progression have been entirely rebuilt.

* **RPG Attributes & Progression:** You have core RPG stats (like strength, agility, etc.) that you can build upon (**RPG Attribute System**). Your build matters.
* **Overhauled Combat:** The feel of combat is completely different. Expect revised attack animations, tweaked cooldowns, and more fluid, impactful weapon swings (**Combat Reborn** & **Combat Plus**).
* **Loot & Enchanting:** Expect new, highly specialized RPG enchantments (**RPG Enchantments**) and a completely revamped enchanting and expedition system (**Enchants and Expeditions** & **Extended Enchanting**).

---

<h2 style="color: #2980B9;">🛡️ 3. Character Progression: The RPG System</h2>

You are no longer just a standard Minecraft character relying entirely on the armor you happen to find. This modpack turns your character into a customizable RPG class.

* **Leveling Up & Stats (`RPG Attribute System`):** As you gain experience, you aren't just saving it for enchantments anymore. You can open a dedicated menu to spend points on core RPG attributes. Want to be a tank? Pump points into Vitality and Defense. Want to be a glass cannon? Max out Strength and Agility. Your build completely changes how you play.
* **Specialized Gear (`RPG Enchantments`):** Vanilla enchantments have been heavily expanded. You will find unique, RPG-style enchants that synergize with your specific stat build, allowing for specific classes like rogues, bruisers, or mages.

---

<h2 style="color: #D35400;">🏘️ 4. The New Village Economy & Quests</h2>

Villages are no longer just places you raid for wheat and beds. They are the central hubs of your adventure, economy, and questing.

* **Bounties & Quests (`Village Quest`, `Pumpkillagers Quest`, `Dark Questing`):** Villagers now need your help. You can pick up quests and bounties to hunt specific monsters, gather resources, or explore dangerous areas in exchange for rare loot, experience, and emeralds.
* **New Professions (`More Villagers`):** The trading economy is massive now. Expect to see brand-new villager professions with custom workstations (like Woodworkers, Oceanographers, or Miners), giving you entirely new ways to trade for materials you actually need.
* **Villages Everywhere (`Moog's Missing Villages`, `Underground Villages`, `End Villager Outpost`):** You will find bustling settlements in biomes that never used to have them. Keep an eye out for hidden underground dwarf-style villages, jungle settlements, and even strange outposts in the End dimension!
* **Immersion & Quality of Life (`Random Villager Names`, `Villager Config`):** Villagers now have actual generated names, making them feel like real NPCs rather than generic trading blocks. The underlying mechanics of how they restock and behave have also been tweaked to make the trading economy fairer and less exploitable.

---

<h2 style="color: #8E44AD;">👹 5. Bosses & Dimensions</h2>

When you're geared up, the real challenges begin.

* **Boss Checklist:** Don't know what to fight next? Use the in-game **Boss Checklist** to track your progression and see which major enemies you've defeated.
* **Naraka:** A massive new dimension/expansion bringing punishing, high-tier bosses and enemies inspired by Eastern mythology and action games.
* **Aerial Hell:** A dangerous, sky-based dimension filled with unique biomes, aggressive mobs, and its own set of bosses.
* **Legacies & Legends:** A massive content addition featuring new legendary weapons, ancient enemies, and formidable bosses scattered throughout the world.
* **The True Ending:** The End dimension isn't just a dragon fight anymore. It has been completely overhauled with new challenges and a proper "true" conclusion to the dimension (**Tru.e Ending** & **End Reborn**).
* **Tougher Overworld Threats:** Beware of improved mob AI (**Mob AI Tweaks**), invading factions (**Illager Invasion**), and deadlier spiders (**Stormie Spiders**).

---

<h2 style="color: #27AE60;">🌾 6. Food, Farming & Storage</h2>

With seasons affecting your crops, optimizing your agriculture is crucial.

* **The Farmer's Delight Ecosystem:** Cooking is a massive part of the game now. You have access to pots, skillets, and cutting boards to make hearty meals that provide powerful buffs (**Farmer's Delight**, **Chef's Delight**, **Rustic Delight**, etc.).
* **Farming Quality of Life:** You can harvest and instantly replant crops with a simple right-click (**Right Click Harvest**), and you can utilize 3x3 farming tools for massive yields (**Farm 3x3**).
* **Tom's Simple Storage:** Forget messy chests. You can build a centralized, searchable storage network terminal to access all your items from one block (**Tom's Storage**).

---

<h2 style="color: #F39C12;">🎒 7. Essential Quality of Life (Know Before You Go)</h2>

* **Proximity Chat:** We are using **Simple Voice Chat**. Make sure to bind your push-to-talk key and mute key in the settings so you can talk to people nearby!
* **JourneyMap:** Press `J` (default) to open your fullscreen map. You can set waypoints to your base, view the topography, and see nearby entities.
* **Inventory Expansion:** You can craft a functional backpack to expand your inventory on the go (**Backpacked**), and even equip your tamed wolves with saddlebags (**Wolf Saddle Bag**).
* **Tree Felling:** Don't waste time breaking every log. Chop the bottom block of a tree, and the whole thing will come tumbling down (**FallingTree**).
* **Visual Enhancements:** The world looks significantly better with dynamic rendering, better animations, and particle effects (**GeckoLib**, **Particle Rain**, **Iris** for shaders, and heavily optimized chunk rendering via **Sodium/Lithium**).

---

<h2 style="color: #16A085;">⌨️ 8. Essential Keyboard Shortcuts & Keybinds</h2>

With this many mods, your keyboard is going to be packed. Here are the most important default shortcuts you need to know.

<h3 style="color: #34495E;">🗣️ Communication & Multiplayer</h3>

* **Voice Chat Menu (`V`):** Opens the Simple Voice Chat settings. **Crucial first step:** Use this menu to select your microphone and bind a "Push-to-Talk" or "Mute" key!
* **Ping Wheel (`Middle Mouse Button` / `Mouse Button 5`):** Quickly ping a location, enemy, or item for your friends to see on their screens.

<h3 style="color: #34495E;">🗺️ Navigation & World</h3>

* **Open Full Map (`J`):** Opens the JourneyMap interface where you can view explored areas, manage waypoints, and change map settings.
* **Quick Waypoint (`B`):** Instantly creates a waypoint at your current location so you don't lose your base. *(Note: This often conflicts with other mods!)*

<h3 style="color: #34495E;">🎒 Inventory & Crafting</h3>

* **How to Craft (`R`):** Hover your mouse over *any* item in your inventory or the REI menu on the right, and press **R** to see its recipe.
* **What is this used for? (`U`):** Hover over an item and press **U** to see every recipe that item is used in.
* **Search Bar Highlight (`Double-Click Search Bar`):** Double-click the search bar at the bottom right of your inventory and type an item name. It will darken everything in your chests except the item you are looking for.
* **Inventory Sorting (`Middle Mouse Button`):** Click the middle mouse button while looking at a messy chest or your own inventory to instantly sort it.

> <span style="color: #C0392B;">**⚠️ A Quick Warning on Conflicts!**</span>
> Because we have mods for backpacks, maps, and voice chat, keys like **B**, **V**, and **M** usually have overlapping commands. If a shortcut isn't working, search for that letter in your Key Binds menu and change the ones you don't use to something else (like the numpad)!

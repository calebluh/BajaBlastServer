# 🌾 Village Quest

**By TopasMusic**

Village Quest adds a robust, long-term progression system built entirely around villages, quests, reputation, and unlocks. Instead of just treating villages as quick trading stops, this mod makes them living hubs that you actively invest in over time.

---

## ✨ Features & Overview

At the heart of the mod is the **Questmaster**, a new NPC who handles all of your task boards and progression. As you help a village through regular work, you will slowly build trust, earn reputation, and unlock more of the mod's deeper content.

### 📜 Questing System
The Questmaster offers multiple tiers of quests to keep you busy:
* **Daily Quests:** Quick, repeatable tasks for steady reputation and minor rewards.
* **Weekly Quests:** Larger tasks that require more investment but yield greater payouts.
* **Story Quests:** Narrative-driven missions that advance the lore and your standing in the world.
* **Special Quests:** Unique, challenging objectives that unlock major milestones.

### 📈 Reputation & Unlocks
Progression goes far beyond simple repeatable quests. As your reputation grows, you will unlock:
* **Story Arcs:** Deep, multi-part questlines.
* **Village Projects:** Massive undertakings to improve and expand the settlement.
* **Relic Rewards:** Rare, powerful artifacts earned through dedication.
* **Magical Tools:** Specialized equipment to aid in your adventures and tasks.

---

## 🧳 The Pilgrim (Outer-World Progression)

In addition to the core village loop, the mod includes **The Pilgrim**. This provides a separate, completely optional progression path focused on roadside encounters and outer-world exploration. 

The Pilgrim is designed to expand the world *around* the main village loop, giving nomadic explorers and adventurers meaningful long-term goals outside the safety of the town borders.

---

## 🎯 Who is this for?

Village Quest is meant for players who enjoy:
* Steady, rewarding progression systems.
* Deepening their immersion in "village life."
* Having meaningful, long-term goals to work toward rather than just surviving day-to-day.
